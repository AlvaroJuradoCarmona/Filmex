Hello there!

We are a new chain of cinema, funded in 2021 (and definitely not a project for the course of Web Design at VUT). We know, bad year to start a new cinema with the pandemic and all that, but hey, maybe if everybody follows the sanitary indications wearing a mask and using sanitizer in their hands, we can come back to normality and, hopefully, get a bit drunk from smelling the alcohol in the sanitizer and enjoy our films a bit more (specially Venom 2, you're gonna like to be a little drunk).

We have cinemas in 4 cities in the Czech Republic (or is it Czechia? I never got that clear): the ones you know (Prague and Brno), the one you know from the beer (Plzeň), and the one you don't know (Olomouc). But you know, you can see that info in the 'theaters' part of the website, can't you?

Anyways, come and enjoy our cinemas. Firstly, because cinema is a heart-warming experience in which a group of total strangers become one in order to feel collective feelings based not only in the movie but your personal experience; but mainly, because we need the money. Thanks!
